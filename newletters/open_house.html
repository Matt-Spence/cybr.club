Howdy!
 
The Cybersecurity Club will be hosting an Open House in our Discord Server this Friday at 7 pm, where our officers will be competing in Google CTF, a cybersecurity-focused competition. Our officers will be streaming as a way to teach and demonstrate the kinds of skills you will learn as a member of the club. We will also have other officers available to answer any questions about the club
 
We are A&M's premier cybersecurity student organization, and our goal is to connect you with a career in this exciting and fast-growing field. If you are interested in learning about Cybersecurity, there is a place for you in our organization.

Please join our Discord server by going to the following link to participate and receive future updates:
https://discord.gg/nCpZzbB
 
  • If you are new to cyber, or just curious about exploring this potential career path, we can help you get your foot in the door and set you up for success in this industry.
  • If you have some experience, you may be interested in competing alongside our competition team and representing A&M in competitions around the country.
  • You may also be interested in joining our chapter of Women In Cybersecurity (WiCyS), where we host talks with women in prominent industry positions.
Some of the benefits of membership include:
  • Learning opportunities at our bi-weekly meetings.
  • Networking and recruitment opportunities with our corporate and government partners.
  • The chance to get hands-on experience as part of our competition team.
  • The chance to meet some of the most fun and interesting people on campus.
We do not charge dues, we have no restrictions on majors, and we do not have any formal attendance requirements. The only thing you need to do to join is to sign up for our mailing list and show up ready to learn. Our first meeting will be Tuesday, September 1st at 6:30 pm.
